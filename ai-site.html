Application Manager

When I created this site, I still had minimal experience with web development and React, which is the first JavaScript framework I learned. I, along with 2 other TAs, build this website in a matter of days, having to collaborate on specific design choices while I juggled getting used to React.

Most Computer Science courses at Brown have a dedicated course page, and it is almost always created by the TAs. Since I wanted to get more experience with web development, I volunteered to assist in making the site. We had to collaborate on making the design cohesive after the class voted on a Game of Thrones theme. This involved pictures, fonts, animations, and even text content.

This is just a single page in React, but having to get used to the framework so quickly laid the ground for me to pick up Ember within a matter of days at my next internship, and learning Vue on my own as a passion project.
